Taj Mahal

The renowned Taj Mahal was brought to life by the vision of the Mughal Emperor Shah Jahan. He got this monument built for his beloved wife, Mumtaz Mahal after she passed away. To honor the memory of his loving wife, Shah Jahan ordered the finest artisans from all over the world to build it.

  • Year of construction: From 1632 to 1653.
  • Cost of building: ~ 32 million Indian Rupees.
  • Built by: Shah Jahan (the Mogul emperor)
  • Architect: Ustad Ahmad Lahauri.
  • Location: Agra, Uttar Pradesh, India.
  • Entrance fee: 1000 Rupees for foreigners.
